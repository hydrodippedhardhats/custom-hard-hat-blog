Skull Hard Hats — Every Dark Design We Carry

Not every worker wants an American flag or a carbon fiber finish. Some want something darker — a hard hat that matches the edge they bring to the job site every day. Skull hard hats have been a consistent part of job site culture in construction, oil and gas, and the trades for years. Here's every skull and dark design we carry.

Who Buys Skull Hard Hats

Skull hard hats are popular across most trades but are especially common in construction, oil and gas, and heavy equipment operation. Workers who favor this style tend to have strong occupational identity and want their gear to match their personality. They're also a popular gift — the person who already has everything often doesn't have a skull hard hat.

Job Site Considerations for Dark Designs

Most general construction and industrial job sites have no restrictions on hard hat designs. A small number of sites — particularly large commercial or government projects — have PPE documentation requirements or neutrality policies. Check with your safety officer if you're unsure. The Pyramex Ridgeline shell underneath is ANSI rated Class C, G, and E regardless of the exterior design.
