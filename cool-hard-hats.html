Why Hydro Dipped Is the Only Way to Do a Cool Hard Hat Right

A cool design on a hard hat only works if it lasts. Stickers peel in summer heat. Vinyl lifts at the edges. Spray paint fades. Hydro dipping bonds the design to the surface of the shell at a chemical level — it doesn't sit on top, it becomes part of it. Under normal job site conditions, a hydro dipped hard hat won't peel, bubble, or fade.

Every hat at HydroDippedHardHats.com is hand-dipped individually on a full brim Pyramex Ridgeline shell — ANSI-certified Class C, G, and E rated. Comfortable enough to wear all day, tough enough for any job site.
